INNOVENTURE TOUR 1.0

An unforgettable day of tech discovery for young minds.

What is the Innoventure Tour?

This isn't just a field trip; it's a journey into the future. We're taking 50 curious kids inside LetiArt, one of the leading game design and animation studios.

They'll see how their favorite games are made, talk to real developers, and participate in a mini "Game Jam" session where they create their own character concepts.
