CHOCOLATE WITH TECH

Discover the sweet science and technology behind everyone's favorite treat.

What is Chocolate with Tech?

It's a delicious dive into STEM! We're exploring how technology transforms simple cocoa beans into the chocolate bars we love.

Kids will learn about the chemistry of tempering, 3D chocolate printing, and sustainable farming tech, all while enjoying some tasty experiments.
