Our Story

From a Classroom Dream to a Tech Academy

Founded in 2018, Child in Tech started as a small after-school club where kids could take apart old computers and see how they worked. Today, we're a leading tech academy helping thousands of children discover the magic of coding, robotics, and design.

We don't just teach syntax; we teach problem-solving, resilience, and collaboration. Our mission is to create a safe, fun, and inspiring environment where technology becomes a tool for creative expression.
