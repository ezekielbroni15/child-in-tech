Our Story

From One Student's Dream to Thousands of Opportunities

What started as being excluded from a tech club in high school became the foundation for Child-In-Tech. Founded in 2022, Child-In-Tech began as a small conference offering tech talks, breakout sessions, and hands-on exhibitions. Today, we're a thriving tech company helping thousands of children explore AI, VR, coding, robotics, design, drones, and more through engaging conferences and tours. We teach problem-solving, resilience, and collaboration, creating a safe, fun, and inspiring space where technology becomes a tool for creative expression.
