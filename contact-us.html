Frequently Asked Questions

You Asked, We Answered! You can swipe through our most frequently asked questions.

Q. What is CIT?
CIT (Child-In-Tech) is one of Africa's leading companies focused on empowering children through interactive, hands-on technology conferences. We're committed to ensuring every child across Africa has the opportunity to thrive in our increasingly digital world.
Q. What age range is required to attend?
CIT welcomes children and teenagers between the ages of 6 and 17. Our programs are tailored to meet learners at every stage, from beginners to more advanced young tech enthusiasts.
Q. Is CIT a non-profit organization?
No, we're a for-profit organization. But our mission has always been to make tech education and conferences accessible to every child, which is why we've offered all our events free of charge. Starting January 2026, we're introducing paid tickets for most of our conferences, though we'll continue hosting free programs as part of our commitment to accessibility. Be sure to watch for our announcements so you can grab tickets for paid events or join us at any of our free sessions!!!
Q. How can my organization partner with CIT?
We're open to partnerships that enhance our participants' tech learning experience through fun, interactive opportunities. Whether you're interested in sponsorship, co-hosting events, or other creative partnerships, we're all ears! Contact us by email or DM, and we'll respond promptly to discuss how we can work together.
