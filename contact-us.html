Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about joining and classes.

Q. What is the student-to-teacher ratio?
We maintain a small class size with a maximum of 8 students per instructor to ensure personalized attention.
Q. Do you offer trial classes?
Yes! Every new student can book a free 45-minute trial session for any of our coding or robotics programs.
Q. What age groups do you teach?
Our programs are designed for children aged 5-16, with curriculum tailored specifically for different developmental stages.
Q. Do we need to bring our own laptop?
We provide all necessary equipment including laptops, tablets, and robotics kits for in-person classes.
